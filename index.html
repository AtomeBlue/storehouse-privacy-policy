Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15 April 2025 · Last Updated: 15 April 2025

1. Who We Are

StoreHouse Demo (“App”, “we”, “us”) is a publicly available demonstration of the commercial StoreHouse Platform. The demo is strictly read‑only and shows sample data only. Companies that license the full version (“Customers”) receive their own private database instance hosted on Google Firebase.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Demo version on Google Play

The demo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from end‑users. No analytics, crash reports, cookies, or device identifiers are recorded. All products, clients, and invoices visible in the demo are fictional and pre‑loaded by AtomBlue7.

2.2 Enterprise version (for Customers)

When a Customer deploys the full system, they may store business data such as employee details, product catalogues, and client invoices. AtomBlue7 acts solely as a processor of that data on behalf of the Customer.

3. How Enterprise Data Is Used

  • Authenticating managers and marketers
  • Displaying warehouse inventory and generating PDF invoices
  • Maintaining the service and performing back‑ups

No Enterprise data is shared with third‑party advertisers or analytics providers.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell personal data. Enterprise data is accessible only to:

  • The Customer’s authorised managers inside their dashboard
  • AtomBlue7 engineers solely for support or maintenance, under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Law‑enforcement agencies only when legally obliged

5. Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit & at rest: TLS 1.2+ for all traffic; AES‑256 for stored files and databases
  • Least‑privilege access controls enforced via Firebase security rules and IAM policies
  • Multi‑factor authentication required for AtomBlue7 staff
  • Regular penetration testing and dependency‑vulnerability scans
  • Daily encrypted back‑ups with 30‑day retention in a separate region

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Demo: no personal data retained.
  • Enterprise: data is stored for the lifetime of the Customer contract. Upon termination, AtomBlue7 deletes all copies within 30 days unless legal obligations require longer retention.

End‑users who wish to access or erase data held by a Customer should contact that Customer directly. AtomBlue7 will assist the Customer in fulfilling any deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Children’s Privacy

StoreHouse is intended for business use and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. International Transfers

Enterprise data is stored on Google Cloud servers, which may be located outside your country. Data transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, the “Last Updated” date will change. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
